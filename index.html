State Sponsored Homophobia

Nobody should have to flee their home because of their sexual orientation or gender identity. Often left as untold stories many Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender or Intersex (LGBTI) people face discrimination, persecution and violence on a daily basis, and often have no choice but to flee their country to seek asylum elsewhere. Others are paralysed by fear and suffer in silence, further compounding their vulnerability.

Rainbow Railroad is a Canadian non-profit organization that helps these lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender individuals escape persecution in their home countries.

The International Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Trans and Intersex Association publishes data on sexual orientation laws in the world. This data exposes the arbitrariness of persecutory laws, and starkly indicate the absence of positive law in most parts of the world. I plan to combine ILGA's data history with Rainbow Railroad's annual reports of refugees.

Combining these datasets over a geographical visualization could lead to insights in areas of focus for Rainbow Railroad, allowing them to set their scope towards oppressive countries, and rescue more individuals from violence, murder, or persecution.
